CryptoCongress.info

CryptoCongress convenes now

CryptoCongress brings together the biggest influencers in the crypto community in a secure voting and messaging service on the Ethereum blockchain.

Read white paper Read the one-pager

Novel on-chain token distribution to proven identities

CryptoCongress' unique distribution scheme allocates two-thirds of tokens to top influencers in the crypto space. (See inital token holders below.) Claim your CryptoCongress tokens by tweeting "#cryptocongress YOUR_ETHEREUM_ADDRESS_HERE"

Propose initiatives and vote on the blockchain

CryptoCongress uses Ethereum smart contracts and event-driven infrastructure to allow token-holders to propose and vote on initiatives such as technical upgrades and to counter potential cyber attacks. More tokens = more votes.

Secure messaging and auxilliary services

CryptoCongress exposes proposal and voting APIs for use by web and mobile apps provides open and safe and encrypted communications links between known VIPs of the cryptocurrency phenomenon.

Fair, unique, and simple token distribution scheme

Initial token recipients and crypto influencer rankings


The 1,000 members below comprise the initial tokenholders and the first convention of CryptoCongress. These initial members were determined using an impartial algorithm calculating an infuencer score based on quality Twitter followers.

  • Tokenholders with greater than 800 tokens may issue proposals.
  • Tokenholders with greater than 200 tokens may vote on proposals.
  • Read more about the selection of initial tokenholders on the CryptoCongress white paper.

On the list? Congratulations, you can claim your tokens by tweeting "#cryptocongress YOUR_ETHEREUM_ADDRESS".
